Simu Liu

Simu Liu (born April 19, 1989, Harbin) is a Canadian actor, writer, and stuntman.
He is known for his performance as Jung Kim in the award-winning CBC Television sitcom Kim's Convenience.[1] He received nominations at the ACTRA Awards and Canadian Screen Awards for his work in Blood and Water.[2] He will portray Shang-Chi in the upcoming Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) film Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings (2021). He is the first Asian actor to lead a Marvel Studios film.
